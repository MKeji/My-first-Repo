TOM Brown

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of Groundnut (peanuts)
  • 7 cups of guinea corn
  • 7 cups of soya beans
  • 1 cup of cray fish
  • 1 handful of ginger (dried)
  • 2 tbls of kayan kanshi (cloves,)
